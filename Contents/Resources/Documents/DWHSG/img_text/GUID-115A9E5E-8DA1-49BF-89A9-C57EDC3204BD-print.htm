Description of the illustration dwhsg075.eps

This figure illustrates a rollup for a dimension with two hierarchies. In it:

  • year: is at the top of the dimension hierarchy

  • quarter: is below year

  • month: is below quarter

  • day: is at the bottom of the dimension hierarchy

In addition, there is a second rollup. In it:

  • fis_year: is at the top of the dimension hierarchy

  • fis_quarter: is below fis_year

  • fis_month: is below fis_quarter

  • fis_week: is below fis_month

  • day: is at the bottom of the dimension hierarchy

Therefore, day rolls up both hierarchies.
