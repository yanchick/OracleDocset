Description of the illustration dwhsg084.eps

This figure illustrates the example of an In table containing:

  • North 10

  • South 20

  • North 25

  • East 5

These are in columns of Region and Sales. They then go into a table function, which leads to output of:

  • North 35

  • South 30

  • West 10

  • East 5

This is a simple example, and is not meant to illustrate syntax.
