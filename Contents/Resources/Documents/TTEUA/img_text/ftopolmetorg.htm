Description of the illustration ftopolmetorg.gif

This figure shows the Metric Order dialog. Below the title there is a list with your selected metrics. To the right of the metrics list there are arrow buttons to organize your metrics: Move selected items to top of list, Move selected items up one in list, Move selected items down one in list, and Move selected items to bottom of list. At the bottom of the dialog there are the following buttons: OK and Cancel.
