Description of the illustration wizarda.gif

Displays the three steps to perform a database scan:

  • Scan Parameters

    Specifies some details for how the scanning will be performed.

  • Objects Selection

    Defines the scope of what will be scanned.

  • Scan Details

    Displays various aspects of the scan, such as whether ROWIDs will be collected.
