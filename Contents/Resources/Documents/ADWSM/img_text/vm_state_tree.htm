Description of the illustration vm_state_tree.eps

This is a text description of Figure 1-1, "Workspace Tree". It illustrates a workspace tree, with the LIVE workspace at the top. Under the LIVE workspace are its two child workspaces, Workspace1 and Workspace4. Under Workspace1 are its two child workspaces, Workspace2 and Workspace3; and under Workspace4 is its child workspace, Workspace5.

The text before the illustration describes the creation of the workspaces in this tree.
