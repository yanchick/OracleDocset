Description of the illustration upgrade.eps

This figure shows host servers host1 and host2 and an Oracle database.

  • The host1 is shown to host the active1 and standby2 TimesTen databases. The host2 is shown to host the active2 and standby1 TimesTen databases.

  • Replication is shown to go from active1 on host1 to standby1 on host2, and from active2 on host2 to standby2 on host1.

  • AWT updates are shown to go from host1 and host2 to Oracle Database.

  • Read-only updates are shown to go from Oracle Database to host1 and host2.

End of description.
