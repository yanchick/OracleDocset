Description of the illustration repln073.eps

This illustration shows three master sites with the hr_mg master group. The hr_mg master group contains exactly the same objects at each of the three master sites. These objects are the following:

  • hr.employees

  • hr.departments

  • hr.locations

  • hr.countries

  • hr.job_history

  • hr.jobs

  • hr.regions
