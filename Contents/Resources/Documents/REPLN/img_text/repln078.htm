Description of the illustration repln078.eps

This illustration shows two master sites: master site 1 and master site 2. Each master site contains two master groups, Group A and Group B, and the database objects in these replication groups are exactly the same at each master site.

The database objects in Group A at both sites are the following:

  • hr.employees

  • hr.departments

  • hr.locations

  • hr.countries

  • hr.job_history

  • hr.jobs

  • hr.regions

The database objects in Group B at both sites are the following:

  • oe.customers

  • oe.orders

  • oe.order_items

  • oe.product_information

  • oe.product_descriptions

  • oe.inventories

  • oe.warehouses
