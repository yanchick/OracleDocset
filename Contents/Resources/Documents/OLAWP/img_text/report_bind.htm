The report shows the query, SELECT * FROM user_tab_columns WHERE table_name = ?, and the query results:

  • TABLE_NAME column: CUSTOMER_MARKET_VIEW for all rows.

  • COLUMN_NAME column: DIM_KEY, LEVEL_NAME, MEMBER_TYPE, DIM_ORDER, HIER_ORDER, LONG_DESCRIPTION, SHORT_DESCRIPTION, TOTAL_LONG_DESCRIPTION, TOTAL_LONG_DESCRIPTION1, MARKET_SEGMENT_LONG_DESC, MARKET_SEGMENT_LONG_DE_1, ACCOUNT_LONG_DESCRIPTION, ACCOUNT_LONG_DESCRIPTI_1, SHIP_TO_LONG_DESCRIPTION, SHIP_TO_LONG_DESCRIPTI_1, PARENT, DEPTH, TOTAL, CUSTOMER_TOTAL_ID, MARKET_SEGMENT, CUSTOMER_MARKET_SEGMENT_, ACCOUNT, CUSTOMER_ACCOUNT_ID, SHIP_TO, CUSTOMER_SHIP_TO_ID.

  • DATA_TYPE column: NUMBER for DIM_ORDER, HIER_ORDER, and DEPTH, and VARCHAR2 for all other rows.

  • The other columns in the report do not appear in the figure.
