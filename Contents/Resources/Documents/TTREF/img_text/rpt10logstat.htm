Description of the illustration rpt10logstat.gif

This graphic shows log statistics. The value, rate of change, and source (always the SYSTEMSTATS table) is shown for each statistic. Statistics include log.buffer.insertions, log.buffer.waits, log.file.earliest, log.file.latest, log.file.reads, log.file.writes, and log.forces.
