Description of the illustration rpt02stmt.gif

This graphic shows the Statement Statistics section of a ttStats report. The value, rate per second, and source (SYSTEMSTATS table) are shown for numerous statement statistics such as stmt.executes.alters, stmt.executes.deletes, stmt.executes.inserts, and stmt.prepares.count.
