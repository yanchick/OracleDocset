Description of the illustration rpt17autrefcg.gif

This graphic shows auto-refresh cache group statistics. For each statistic, it shows the value, rate of change per second, rate of change per cycle, and source (SYSTEMSTATS table). Statistics include cg.autorefresh.cycles.completed and .failed, cg.autorefresh.deletes.rows and the same for updates and inserts, and cg.autorefresh.full_refreshes.
