Description of the illustration host.gif

The Host section of the Performance page is shown.

A line chart is shown. The y-axis units are Runnable Processes and range from 0 to 4.5. The x-axis units are times, shown in 3-hour intervals. The x-axis values are from 4:40 a.m. to 2:30 a.m. The chart tracks load average for host activity. There is a Maximum CPU line along the x-axis at 2 on the y-axis. The chart shows that activity hovered around 1 for most of the time except for a spike up to 4 at 1:00 p.m. and another spike up to 4.5 at 2:00 p.m. falling gradually down to 1 by 4:30 p.m.

End of description.
