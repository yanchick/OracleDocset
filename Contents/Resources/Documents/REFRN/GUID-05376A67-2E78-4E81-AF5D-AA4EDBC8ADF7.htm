5.280 DBA_SCHEDULER_CREDENTIALS

DBA_SCHEDULER_CREDENTIALS displays information about all credentials in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_SCHEDULER_CREDENTIALS.

Note:

This view is deprecated in favor of the DBA_CREDENTIALS view. Oracle recommends that you use DBA_CREDENTIALS instead. DBA_SCHEDULER_CREDENTIALS is retained for backward compatibility only.
