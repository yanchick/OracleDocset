Description of the illustration disprop.eps

This image illustrates measure populating with disproportional assigned measures, as described in text before and after the illustration. The assigned measure values (associated with small white circles) are proportional to their relative distances between the original disproportional measure values that are not changed (associated with small shaded circles), but they are not proportional to relative distances across the entire geometric segment.
